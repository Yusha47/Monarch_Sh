Notes

Virtual contests will give you a real-time experience. It will enable the users to run the past contests in a special mode that would imitate a real competition. It feels just like a real contest with real contestants competing alongside the participant who writes a virtual contest.

Tips

If you can't solve a problem, then move on to another, maybe you will find that one easier. Don't take too much time on a single problem. You can attack it later after solving other problems which might be easier for you.

Also, don't panic. Cool down and remember that you can do it. If you are not getting AC, just accept that the problem is from your side. So cool down, read the problem again and try to debug and get AC.

Good Luck.
